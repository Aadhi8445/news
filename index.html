MS Dhoni's World Cup Final Bat Entered The Guinness Book Of World Records In 2011.

The moment is still glorified in every Indian cricket fan's mind, with Dhoni sending a Nuwan Kulasekara delivery soaring for a six, and then standing still for a few seconds with his bat raised.

The moment was so iconic that months later, the bat was sold for a record amount of 100,000 pounds (One crore rupees approximately) on July 18, 2011. It is also listed by the Guinness World Records as the most expensive cricket bat.
